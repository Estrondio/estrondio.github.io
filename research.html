The Go/NoGo Task

The Go/NoGo task is a behavioral task used in psychology and neuroscience to assess an individual's ability to inhibit responses. It involves presenting stimuli (cues) and requires the participant to respond in a specific way based on the nature of the cue.

In the Go/NoGo task, a "Go" cue signals the participant to perform a certain action, while a "NoGo" cue requires the participant to withhold that action. This task is valuable in understanding cognitive functions related to response inhibition and impulsivity.
